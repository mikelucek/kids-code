Bella

Bella, a border collie from San Diego, Ca didn't even know how to turn her computer on at the start of the program. By week 2, Bella learned to bark repeatedly at the computer until her owners turned it on for her. Bella hopes to develop a site that allows dogs to rate parks, fire hydrants, and dog food brands.
