Rusty, a four year old German Shepard from Portland, Oregon studied web design, learning html and CSS. After only one week, Rusty learned how to tap his paw repeatedly on the keyboard to get a treat as a reward.

Week two Rusty moved into more advanced techniques. However, Rusty expressed frusturation and chewed the keyboard.
